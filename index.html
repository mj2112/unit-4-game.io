Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random nummber at the start of the game.

Ther are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random target number displayed. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
